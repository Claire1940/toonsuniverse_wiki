## The "Motivate" Strategy for Farming Malachor

If you want to unlock all **toons universe agents** quickly, you need to understand the power of elevator cards. Specifically, the "Motivate" and "Job Well Done" cards are the most significant factors in your earning potential. While "Job Well Done" rewards those who focus on machines, "Motivate" is widely considered the superior choice for group play and general farming.

The "Motivate" card increases the Malachor yield from every capsule picked up by any player in the session. Since capsules are far more numerous than machines, the cumulative bonus from "Motivate" far outstrips other buffs.
