## Agent Koi: Core Stat Overview

Koi is widely recognized for her balanced but powerful stat distribution. Unlike specialized agents who may excel in one area while faltering in others, she provides a reliable performance across almost all primary functions. This makes her an excellent choice for players who prefer a "jack-of-all-trades" approach.

### Base Stat Distribution

| Attribute | Value (Bars) | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extraction** | 5 | High efficiency for objective completion |
| **Speed** | 4 | Excellent mobility for kiting and escape |
| **Stamina** | 3 | Moderate pool for diving and running |
| **Hacking** | 3 | Capable of handling standard tech tasks |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Because Koi has a low Sanity stat, prioritize items or passive buffs that mitigate sanity loss during blackouts or encounters with high-threat infected.

## Understanding Abilities: Sheer and Dive

To maximize your impact, you must master the synergy between Koi’s passive and active abilities. These skills define her role as both a primary objective runner and a tactical survivor.

### Passive Ability: Sheer
The "Sheer" passive provides a full cooldown reset on the nearest player whenever Koi completes a machine. While the exact range and scope of this effect can be situational, it is most effective when coordinated with teammates. By timing your machine completions, you can effectively refresh your team's tactical abilities, allowing for continuous pressure or rapid escapes.

### Active Ability: Dive
The "Dive" ability allows Koi to submerge into the ground, granting temporary invincibility. This is her primary survival tool. However, it is heavily dependent on her current stamina pool.

*   **Invincibility Duration:** Scales based on current Stamina.
*   **Cooldown:** 75 seconds.
*   **Strategic Use:** Use this to bypass infected, avoid damage during high-intensity events, or to "pseudo-stun" enemies by breaking their pathing AI.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using "Dive" when your stamina is low. If you initiate the ability without sufficient stamina, you may find yourself vulnerable immediately upon surfacing, leaving you unable to sprint away from a chase.

## Advanced Gameplay Strategies

To excel with **Toons Universe Koi**, you need to treat the game as a team-based endeavor. While her extraction speed is top-tier, her true power lies in her ability to manipulate the flow of the match.

### Efficient Objective Management
With 5 bars of extraction, Koi should always be the one prioritizing machine repairs. When playing on higher floors (Floor 10+), focus on clearing machines quickly to trigger your passive reset. Always pair this with items like the USB drive or gloves to speed up your interaction time, ensuring you aren't stuck holding the lever for longer than necessary.

### Kiting and Pseudo-Stunning
During an infected pursuit, use the "Dive" ability not just for invincibility, but to reposition. By diving just as an infected approaches, you can often force them to reset their pathing, essentially "pseudo-stunning" them. This creates the perfect window for your teammates to finish their tasks or for you to reach the next safe zone.

### Team Synergy
Koi pairs exceptionally well with agents specialized in Hacking (like Guido and Rozum). While they handle the tech, you should focus on gathering resources and completing physical objectives. If you find yourself in a match without a dedicated hacker, use your 3-bar hacking stat to support them, but be aware that you will be slower than a specialized agent.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Toons Universe Koi the best agent for beginners?**

A: She is definitely one of the strongest choices. Her high extraction speed and mobility make her forgiving to play, though players must learn to manage her Stamina consumption for her "Dive" ability to survive longer runs.

**Q: How does Koi’s passive interact with team cooldowns?**

A: Her passive "Sheer" resets the cooldown of the nearest player's active ability upon completing a machine. To get the most out of this, communicate with your team so they can use their abilities right before you finish a machine, maximizing their uptime.

**Q: What is the biggest weakness of playing as Koi?**

A: Her primary weakness is her low Sanity stat. Players must be extremely cautious during "Blackout" events or when facing enemies that drain Sanity, as she lacks the natural resistance that other agents might possess.
