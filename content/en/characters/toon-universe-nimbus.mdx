Unlike other agents whose stats are easily visible, the starters in Toon Universe often require a deeper look into the game's code to understand their true potential. Nimbus excels in physical endurance but struggles slightly with technical tasks.

| Attribute | Rating | Points/Value | Impact |

In Toon Universe, there is a clear distinction between **Extraction** and **Hacking**. Extraction governs the vats with levers, while Hacking governs the computers with circle checks. Nimbus is significantly more efficient at manual extraction than technical hacking, likely due to his long, cozy sleeves interfering with keyboard work.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hacking Penalty">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Abilities and Sanity Management">

The kit of **Toon Universe Nimbus** revolves around the interplay between his stamina pool and the game's unique Sanity mechanic. As you are chased, your sanity drops, which usually cripples your stamina regeneration. Nimbus has two ways to bypass this.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Passive: Haze">
    - **Trigger**: Stamina falls below 50% (100 points).
    - **Effect**: Movement speed increases by **30%**.
    - **Benefit**: Allows you to outrun almost any infected once the chase gets intense.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active: Power Nap">
    - **Execution**: Falls asleep standing up for 5 seconds.
    - **Effect**: Restores **100% stamina** instantly.
    - **Cooldown**: 30 seconds.
    - **Risk**: You are completely immobile while napping.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="The Sanity Loop">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Activity} title="Solo Survival Strategy">

Mastering **Toon Universe Nimbus** requires a rhythmic approach to movement. You want to dance around the 50% stamina mark to keep your speed buff active without leaving yourself vulnerable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Aggressive Scouting">
    Use your high base speed to find vats early. Don't be afraid to sprint, as your **Haze** passive will kick in to help you escape if you stumble upon an infected.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Controlled Sanity Loss">
    During a chase, your sanity will drop. Instead of trying to hide immediately, use your 30% speed boost from **Haze** to lead the infected away from mission objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Tactical Nap">
    Once you have broken line of sight, immediately find a closet or a dark corner. Use **Power Nap**. By the time the infected finds you again, you will have a full 200-point stamina bar ready.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extraction Focus">
    Prioritize vats. Since your Extraction stat is higher than Hacking, you will contribute more to the team (or solo progress) by sticking to the levers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Future Gear">
While the Emblem system is not yet fully implemented in the beta, look for equipment that boosts **Hacking** to balance his weaknesses or **Sanity Resistance** to extend his chase time.
</Callout>

</Section>
