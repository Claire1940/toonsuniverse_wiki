## Selena’s Core Statistics and Profile

Selena is often described as a "classy" agent, a sentiment reflected in her refined character model and her specialized stat distribution. Unlike bruisers or generalist characters, her stats are heavily skewed toward mental fortitude and technical proficiency. In the **toons universe sunny** environment, these attributes make her the go-to choice for hacking computers while maintaining a cool head under pressure.

| Statistic | Value | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Speed** | 3/5 | Average mobility; enough to reposition but not to outrun everyone. |
| **Stamina** | 3/5 | Standard endurance for sprinting and performing physical tasks. |
| **Extracting** | 1/5 | Her weakest point; she is exceptionally slow at manual extraction. |
| **Hacking** | 4/5 | Top-tier technical skill; she clears computer objectives rapidly. |

Because her extracting stat is so low, players should avoid soloing extraction machines unless absolutely necessary. Her "brittle wings" are metaphorically and literally not built for heavy labor. Instead, focus on finding the PCs scattered across the map, where her level 4 hacking can shine. Her high sanity is a massive boon, allowing her to stay in the field longer without succumbing to the psychological pressures that plague lower-sanity agents.

## Mastering the Toons Universe Sunny Recon Meta

The defining characteristic of Selena is her ability to see what others cannot. In a game where the Infected can hide around any corner, her dual-layer scanning system provides a safety net for the entire team. Mastering the **toons universe sunny** recon meta requires a deep understanding of her passive and active abilities, as well as their respective cooldowns.

### Echolocation (Passive)
Selena’s passive ability, Echolocation, automatically triggers every 25 seconds. For a duration of 5 seconds, all living life forms are highlighted. This is an "always-on" utility that ensures Selena is rarely surprised by a roaming Infected. 

> **💡 Tip:** Pay attention to the internal clock of Echolocation. If you know a scan is coming up in 5 seconds, wait behind cover before making a dash for a new objective.

### Sonar (Active)
Her active ability, Sonar, is a more powerful, manual version of her passive. When activated, it highlights all Infected, items, and objectives for 5 seconds. This ability has a 40-second cooldown, making it a strategic tool rather than something to be spammed.

## Hacking Strategy and Objective Priority

In any **toons universe sunny** match, the division of labor is the key to success. Selena should almost always be the designated "Hacker." Computers are her natural habitat, and her high hacking stat ensures that these objectives are completed much faster than the manual extraction points.

When you begin a floor, your first action should be to use Sonar to locate all nearby PCs. Because her extraction speed is a meager 1/5, you are essentially wasting time if you sit on an extraction machine while a PC is available. If you are forced to work on an extractor, try to find items like "Mints" or "Coffee" in the shop to temporarily boost your efficiency.

| Objective Type | Selena's Efficiency | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computers (PCs)** | **High (4/5)** | Primary focus; prioritize these above all else. |
| **Extraction Machines** | **Low (1/5)** | Avoid unless assisted by a teammate with high extraction. |
| **Fuses/Levers** | **Medium** | Use Sonar to locate them for teammates, only interact if safe. |
| **Sanity Management** | **Excellent (5/5)** | Stay near objectives longer; you don't need to retreat as often. |

One of the most rewarding aspects of playing Selena is hitting the "Blue Skill Checks." While hacking, landing the cursor on the blue section of the bar provides a significant progress boost. Given her high hacking stat, these bars often move at a pace that is manageable for experienced players, allowing for "perfect" runs that can finish a PC objective in record time.

## Team Synergy: Working with "Peasants"

Selena is a force multiplier, but she is rarely a solo carry. Her design encourages cooperation. In the **toons universe sunny** gameplay loop, she acts as the "eyes" of the team, while characters like Fizzle or GNR act as the "brawn."

If you are playing with a teammate who has high extraction stats, your job is to scan the area and clear the computers while they handle the heavy machinery. Use your voice lines or pings to alert them to the location of the Infected (like Patcher or Discard). 

> **Warning:** If your teammate leaves the match or is eliminated, your progress will slow down significantly. Selena struggles in solo "clutch" situations due to her inability to clear extraction machines quickly.

## Advanced Tactics: The "Rouge" Playstyle

Experienced players often refer to a high-level Selena as "Code Name Rouge." This playstyle involves using her high sanity and stealth capabilities to remain deep in "enemy" territory while the rest of the team stays near the elevator. Because Discard and other Infected rely on detection, Selena’s ability to see them through walls allows her to dance around their patrol routes.

1.  **Wall Scanning**: You can activate Sonar while hiding behind walls to see if an Infected is camping an objective.
2.  **Progress Tracking**: In recent 2026 updates, scanning now shows the completion progress of objectives. Use this to determine if a PC is worth finishing or if it's better to move to a fresh one.
3.  **Sanity Buffs**: Since your base sanity is 5, items that provide sanity resistance are less critical for you. Instead, spend your Malachor on speed-boosting items like "Socks" or "Coffee" to compensate for your average base speed.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Selena viable for solo play in Toons Universe Sunny?**

A: While it is possible to survive solo due to her high sanity and recon abilities, it is not recommended. Her 1/5 extraction stat makes clearing the final objectives extremely tedious. She is best played in a duo or full squad.

**Q: Does Selena's Sonar highlight items as well as enemies?**

A: Yes, the 2026 version of Sonar highlights Infected, objectives (PCs and extractors), and useful items like bandages or Malachor piles.

**Q: How do I counter the "Discard" Infected while playing as Selena?**

A: Use your passive Echolocation to track Discard's movement. Since he has a roar that can detect players, your best bet is to use your high sanity to stay calm and move out of his radius before he activates his global scan.

**Q: Which items should I prioritize in the shop for Selena?**

A: Focus on **Socks** for increased speed and **Coffee** for stamina. Since her hacking is already high, you don't necessarily need technical buffs, but anything that helps her move between PCs faster is a top priority.
