## Toons Universe Fizzle: Agent Overview & Stats

Fizzle is often compared to characters like Pebble due to his aggro-centric gameplay, but he brings a unique flair to the "distractor" archetype. His base stats are heavily skewed toward movement, making him one of the most agile characters in the game. However, this mobility comes at a significant cost to his objective-handling capabilities.

| Attribute | Rating | Gameplay Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Speed** | **Maximum** | Allows for easy kiting and rapid map traversal. |
| **Stamina** | **High** | Essential for long-distance sprints away from fast infected. |
| **Hacking** | **Very Low** | Progresses objectives at a significantly reduced rate. |
| **Extraction** | **Very Low** | Not recommended for solo machine work. |
| **Support** | **Excellent** | High utility in saving teammates from being chased. |

Because Fizzle has such low ratings in hacking and extraction (often cited as a "2" in both categories), players should avoid staying on machines for extended periods. Instead, your time is better spent searching for capsules and fuses. His "pet-type" animations, where he mimics the standing postures of other agents, add a layer of personality to his role as a supportive companion on the field.

> **💡 Tip:** Use Fizzle to scout ahead and grab research capsules. His speed allows him to enter high-risk areas and exit before the infected can effectively corner him.

## Mastering Toons Universe Fizzle: Ability Mechanics

The core of playing **toons universe fizzle** effectively revolves around his active ability. This skill is a double-edged sword that provides immense utility but carries a heavy penalty if mismanaged. When activated, Fizzle makes himself "Physical Exposed," which serves several functions simultaneously.

### The Aggro Pull
The primary function of the ability is to attract all nearby infected for a duration of 10 seconds. During this window, any infected chasing your teammates will break their current pursuit and pivot toward Fizzle. This is invaluable when an ally is low on health or stuck in a dead-end hallway.

### The Stamina Penalty
While the ability grants you the attention of the hoard, it also triggers a higher stamina drain for 5 seconds. This means you must have a clear exit strategy or a "looping spot" in mind before you hit the button. Popping the ability with an empty stamina bar is a common mistake that leads to immediate capture.

| Ability Aspect | Duration | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Infected Attraction** | **10 Seconds** | Forces infected to target Fizzle within a wide radius. |
| **Stealth Reduction** | **Active** | Lowers stealth, making the agent highly visible. |

## Kiting Strategy and Map Control

To play **toons universe fizzle** at a high level, you must adopt a support mindset. Your goal is to be the "shield" for your hackers. By using your ability near the main objective machines, you can lure infected like Nimbus or G&R away from the area, giving your team the breathing room they need to finish the floor.

1. **Positioning:** Stay on the periphery of the objective zone.
2. **Activation:** Wait until an infected enters the room or an ally is highlighted as "being chased."
3. **The Lure:** Activate the ability and lead the infected toward a different section of the map.
4. **The Escape:** Use stealth items or mint candies to recover stamina once the ability's drain period ends.

It is important to note that distracting is inherently dangerous in Toons Universe. Certain enemies, such as Copper, are known to have unpredictable pathfinding that can lead to players being hit through walls or in the middle of a loop. Always maintain a "buffer zone" between yourself and the infected.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be extremely careful when using your ability against "Typo." This infected is exceptionally fast and can slow you down, making it nearly impossible to escape if you are already under the stamina drain penalty.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Fizzle good for solo play in Toons Universe?**

A: Fizzle is generally considered a poor choice for solo play. Because his hacking and extraction stats are so low, completing objectives takes significantly longer, increasing the chance of being overwhelmed by infected. He is much more effective in a coordinated group.

**Q: Does Fizzle's ability work on all infected?**

A: Yes, the ability attracts all standard infected on the field. However, its effectiveness varies. It is excellent for leading slow enemies like Patrick away, but very risky against fast enemies like Typo who can close the gap quickly during your stamina drain window.

**Q: How do I unlock the best voice lines for **toons universe fizzle**?**

A: Voice lines and unique animations are typically tied to character progression and obtaining specific emblems. As you complete more runs and reach floor five consistently, you will unlock more of his "mimicry" personality traits.

**Q: Should I ever work on the machines as Fizzle?**

A: Only if no one else is available or the machine is nearly finished. Fizzle is much more useful staying mobile, gathering fuses, and keeping the area clear for high-stat hackers. If you do work on a machine, consider using a Mint Candy to boost your efficiency temporarily.
