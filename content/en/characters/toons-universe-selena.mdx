Selena is designed as a high-intel, high-sanity agent. Her stats reflect a character who is meant to stay in the fray longer without losing her composure, though her physical strength for certain tasks is limited. In the 2026 version of Toons Universe, her hacking remains her primary interactive strength.

## Ability Breakdown: Echolocation and Sonar

What truly defines **toons universe selena** is her ability to gather information. She possesses both a passive and an active ability that make her the ultimate scout.

### Passive: Echolocation
Selena’s passive ability, Echolocation, triggers automatically every 25 seconds. For a duration of 5 seconds, all living life forms in her vicinity are revealed. This is an incredible tool for avoiding ambushes and keeping tabs on teammate movements without needing to expend active cooldowns.

### Active: Sonar
Her active ability, Sonar, is a manual scan that highlights infected, items, and objectives temporarily. This is the "bread and butter" of her kit, allowing the team to find fuses, medkits, and extraction points instantly.

| Ability Feature | Echolocation (Passive) | Sonar (Active) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Duration** | 5 Seconds | 5 Seconds |
| **Cooldown** | 25 Seconds (Auto) | 40 Seconds |
| **Targets Revealed** | Living Life Forms | Infected, Items, Objectives |
| **Range** | Moderate Radius | Global/Large Area |

## Strategic Gameplay and Team Synergy

Playing Selena effectively requires a shift in mindset from aggressive objective-rushing to supportive reconnaissance. Because she is "not built" for extraction, she relies heavily on "peasants" (higher extraction stat teammates) to handle the heavy machinery while she clears the path.

### The Hacker Role
With a hacking stat of 4, Selena should always be the one stationed at computers. In the 2026 maps, PC objectives often hold the key to opening new areas or completing soul file uploads. When paired with items like the "Mint," her hacking speed becomes nearly instantaneous, allowing her to finish tasks before the infected can even close the distance.

### Team Compositions
Selena shines brightest when paired with agents who can protect her or handle the tasks she dislikes.

*   **Selena + Fizzle:** A balanced duo where Selena handles the intel and hacking, while Fizzle manages the extraction machines.
*   **Selena + GNR:** GNR provides the muscle and distraction, allowing Selena to use her Sonar to guide the team toward the final elevator.
*   **Selena + Fantasia:** A high-utility team that focuses on map control and status effects.

| Objective Type | Selena's Priority | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computers (PCs)** | **Critical** | Use Sonar to check for nearby infected before starting. |
| **Extraction Machines** | **Low** | Only engage if a teammate is already working on it. |
| **Fuses/Items** | **Support** | Scan and ping locations for teammates to pick up. |

## FAQ

**Q: Is toons universe selena good for solo play?**

A: While it is possible to solo with Selena due to her high sanity and recon abilities, it is not recommended. Her very low extraction stat makes completing certain objectives extremely tedious and time-consuming. She is much more effective as part of a coordinated team.

**Q: Does Selena's Sonar reveal the infected through walls?**

A: Yes, her active Sonar ability highlights infected, items, and objectives through walls and obstacles for 5 seconds. This allows her to scout entire rooms before entering them.

**Q: What is the difference between Echolocation and Sonar?**

A: Echolocation is a passive ability that triggers automatically every 25 seconds and only shows "living life forms" (teammates and certain NPCs). Sonar is an active ability with a 40-second cooldown that highlights everything important, including the infected and mission-critical items.

**Q: Did they change Selena's model in the 2026 update?**

A: Yes, Selena received a significant visual overhaul in the 2026 release. Her model is more detailed, her animations are smoother, and her voice lines have been updated to reflect her "classy" and "upright" personality.
