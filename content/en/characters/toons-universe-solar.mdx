## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more toons universe solar energy during the night?**

A: Currently, the best way to maintain power at night is to invest in high-capacity Battery Cells or use the "Symbiotic Bloom" modules which store a small amount of internal energy within their organic components. You can also head to the Central Atrium, which maintains a baseline power level regardless of the time.

**Q: Does the color of the solar panels affect their performance?**

A: In the 2026 update, cosmetic skins do not affect performance, but different "Tech Tiers" (which have distinct colors like Blue, Gold, and Emerald) have significantly different energy absorption rates. Always check the item stats before prioritizing aesthetics.

**Q: What happens if my Symbiosis Meter reaches zero?**

A: If your symbiosis fails, your **toons universe solar** collectors will begin to "tarnish," reducing their efficiency by up to 50%. You will also notice the local wildlife becoming more defensive. To fix this, plant more native Unova flora and remove any high-pollution machinery.

**Q: Can I share solar power with my friends in multiplayer?**

A: Yes! You can create a "Power Link" by connecting your bases to a shared grid. This allows players with high-output solar farms in the desert to provide energy to friends building in the shaded forest biomes.
