## Understanding Toons Universe Stats and Emblems

In Toons Universe, every agent starts with a base set of attributes. However, these base **toons universe stats** are often insufficient for high-level play. Emblems act as equippable gear that grants passive abilities or stat multipliers. You are allowed to equip up to two Emblems simultaneously, providing a layer of strategic depth that was previously missing from the genre.

Unlike other games where equipment might offer negligible 1% or 2% boosts, Toons Universe Emblems are "genuinely nuts," offering buffs as high as 30% for individual stamina or 15% for the entire team. This design choice ensures that as you invest time into the game, your power level grows tangibly, rewarding skill and dedication.

## How to Unlock Emblems and Boost Stats

The progression system in Toons Universe is straightforward but requires a significant time investment. To unlock an agent's specific Emblem, you must reach **Level 100** with that character. While this sounds daunting, the XP scaling in 2026 is generous, allowing skilled players to gain several levels per successful run.

### XP Gain Methods
You earn experience points by performing virtually any productive action during a match. If you want to maximize your level gains and improve your **toons universe stats** faster, focus on the following activities:

*   **Completing Objectives:** Finishing a computer or an extraction machine provides the largest chunk of XP.
*   **Surviving Floors:** Simply making it to the elevator rewards you for your survival time.
*   **Engaging in Chases:** Successfully distracting the infected and escaping a chase provides "Distraction XP."
*   **Using Items:** Utilizing medkits or speed boosts also contributes small amounts of experience.

## Top Tier Emblems Deep Dive

### The Tadpole Emblem (S-Tier)
Tibo’s Tadpole Emblem is arguably the most powerful item in the game. It increases the team's maximum stamina by 15%. What makes this truly broken is that the effect **stacks**. If a full team of 10 players equips the Tadpole Emblem, the entire group receives a 150% stamina boost. This turns even the slowest agents into marathon runners, making it much easier to kite the infected indefinitely.

### The Bat Emblem (S-Tier)
Selina’s Bat Emblem is the gold standard for objective-focused players. Unlike the Squid Emblem, which only helps with lever-based extractors, the Bat Emblem provides a flat 15% speed increase to **all objectives**, including computers. In a game where every second spent at a machine is a second you are vulnerable, this Emblem is a literal lifesaver.

### The Snake Emblem (A-Tier)
For players who haven't yet memorized the map layouts, the Snake Emblem is a must-have. It highlights machines through walls every minute. While veteran players might eventually find this redundant, it remains a top-tier choice for efficiency, ensuring your team never wastes time wandering around looking for the last objective.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some Emblems, like the Chipmunk Emblem, currently do not trigger the agent's passive abilities (such as Waffle's speed boost). Always check the latest patch notes on the [Official Toons Universe Page](https://www.roblox.com) to see if these interactions have been updated.

## Best Build Strategies for Every Agent

To maximize your **toons universe stats**, you should pair Emblems that complement your agent's natural role. Generally, players fall into two categories: **Distractors** and **Objective Specialists**.

### The Distractor Build
If you are playing as Fizzle or Fantasia, your job is to keep the infected away from the machines.
*   **Primary Emblem:** Sheep Emblem (-30% Stamina Consumption).
*   **Secondary Emblem:** Tadpole Emblem (For the extra stamina pool).
*   **Alternative:** Lizard Emblem (No stamina loss for the first 2 minutes of a floor).

### The Objective Specialist Build
If you are playing as Selina, Discard, or Koi, you want to finish the floor as fast as possible.
*   **Primary Emblem:** Bat Emblem (+15% Objective Speed).
*   **Secondary Emblem:** Snake Emblem (To find machines instantly).
*   **Alternative:** Fish Emblem (If your agent has a high-utility ability like Koi’s Dive).

## FAQ

**Q: Do all Emblems in Toons Universe stack?**

A: Not all of them. The developers have confirmed that the Tadpole Emblem (Team Stamina) stacks, but others like the Snake Emblem (Machine Highlighting) simply refresh the timer rather than providing multiple highlights.

**Q: What are the most important toons universe stats to focus on?**

A: In the current 2026 meta, Stamina and Objective Speed are the most vital. If you can move fast and finish machines quickly, you minimize the time the infected have to corner you.

**Q: Can I use the same Emblem twice on one agent?**

A: No, you must equip two unique Emblems. However, you can equip an Emblem that belongs to a different agent once you have unlocked it by reaching Level 100 with that specific character.

**Q: Is the Bear Emblem better than the Unicorn Emblem for healing?**

A: It depends on your skill level. The Bear Emblem is more reliable for beginners because it guarantees an item every 5 floors. The Unicorn Emblem is better for solo players who are completing a high volume of machines themselves.
