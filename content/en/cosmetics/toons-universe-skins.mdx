## The Value of Supporting Indie Developers

When you purchase **toons universe skins**, you are participating in a micro-economy that sustains independent creators. Unlike major corporations like EA or Activision, which have massive cash reserves and investors, the Toons Universe team relies directly on player contributions.

The developers have even turned down buyout offers from larger companies to maintain their creative vision. If the community is unwilling to support these smaller transactions, indie developers often face two choices: sell the game to a corporation (which usually leads to more aggressive monetization) or stop development entirely.

### Why the $2.00 Price Point Exists

1.  **Platform Fees:** Platforms like Roblox take a significant cut (up to 70%) of every transaction, meaning the developer only sees a fraction of that $2.00.
2.  **Operational Costs:** High-speed internet, professional modeling software, and server hosting are fixed costs that don't decrease just because a player lives in a different region.
3.  **IP Recovery:** The current owners had to pay for the rights to the game, a cost that must be amortized over time through skin sales.
4.  **Living Wages:** Developers need to cover basic necessities like food, housing, and healthcare to continue working on the project.
