## Navigating the 2026 Blackout Difficulty

One of the most controversial changes in the recent update is the adjustment to the Blackout mechanic. The community has noted that Blackouts are significantly darker now, making navigation nearly impossible without a dedicated light source or high-visibility skins. This is where the **toons universe vfx** updates become a double-edged sword; while the effects look better, the darkness makes threats like G&R and Squirtle much more dangerous.

During a Blackout, the flashlights are "barely there," requiring players to rely on environmental knowledge and character-specific glows. Discard’s updated VFX helps slightly, but you must be careful not to get "greedy" with repairs when you can't see the enemy approaching.

### Survival Strategies for Dark Floors:
1.  **Stick with Teepo:** Teepo is often considered a "starter" character due to his balanced stats, but his high stamina makes him a great bodyguard during dark phases.
2.  **Listen for Audio Cues:** Since you can't see G&R as easily, listen for the specific mechanical hum he emits before striking.
3.  **Don't Grade Low Extraction:** It is often "goofy" and dangerous to attempt a high-grade extraction during a Blackout. Focus on completion and moving to the next floor.
4.  **Wrench Investment:** Always keep a Wrench in your inventory. The ability to quickly repair a fuse box can save a run when the lights go out.
