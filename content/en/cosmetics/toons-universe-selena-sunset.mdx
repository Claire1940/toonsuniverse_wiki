## Navigating Threat Levels: G&R and Squiddle

In 2026, the introduction of more complex threats like Gwidon Rusam (G&R) and Squiddle has made the game more dangerous for stationary hackers. Since Selena is often locked into a machine, situational awareness is paramount.

### Dealing with Gwidon Rusam (G&R)
G&R is a particularly nasty threat for Selena because he can occupy holes and surprise players who aren't paying attention to their surroundings. 
1. **Listen for the audio cues:** G&R has a distinct sound when moving between locations.
2. **Scan often:** While Selena’s scan doesn't currently highlight G&R’s specific holes, it allows you to plan your escape route to the next machine before he closes in.
3. **Don't linger:** Once a machine is done, move immediately. Selena is fast, but her defensive stats are low.

### The Problem with Distracting
A common mistake in the current version of the game is players attempting to "distract" the killers without having the proper kit for it. Characters like Selena should almost never be distracting. If you see a Fantasia or a Copper trying to distract Discord or G&R in a bad spot, do not join them. Instead, use that window of time to finish the machines as fast as possible.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid going AFK in the shop or lobby. Modern lobbies in 2026 move fast, and being inactive for even 60 seconds can result in your team losing a crucial player before the first floor is even finished.
