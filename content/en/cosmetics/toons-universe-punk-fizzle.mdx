## Strategy: Navigating the Infected Threats

Playing as Punk Fizzle requires you to be the "eyes and ears" of the team. Because Fizzle is so effective at kiting, you will often find yourself in direct confrontation with the game's various infected. Understanding their unique behaviors is vital.

### High-Threat Targets
*   **Infected Waffle:** Possibly the most dangerous threat. Even with five-star characters, outrunning Waffle is a challenge. If you are playing Fizzle, do not attempt to distract Waffle for long periods; she is simply too fast.
*   **Copper:** A major threat that requires precise dodging. Copper’s movement is erratic, and he can easily cut off your path to the elevator if you aren't careful.
*   **Discord:** Known for his scanning ability. If you get scanned, your location is revealed, and his aggro becomes much harder to shake.

### Sanity Hazards
*   **Squiddle:** While many starters die to Squiddle, he is actually easy to avoid if you pay attention to the indicators. Avoid his tentacles at all costs, as they will devastate your sanity bar.
*   **Patcher:** Hard to see when your sanity is low. Keeping your sanity high via Fizzle's buffed ability is the best way to keep Patcher visible.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never attempt to distract more than two infected at once unless your abilities are off cooldown. Getting cornered by a combination of Copper and Patcher is almost always a death sentence.

## Advanced Tips for Public Lobbies

Public lobbies in [Toon Universe](https://www.roblox.com/games/14546221469/Toon-Universe) can be chaotic, especially when playing with "starters" who may accidentally lead infected toward the main objective. As a Fizzle player, your job is to mitigate this chaos.

1.  **Survey the Map:** Before popping your ability, ensure you have a clear loop path. Look for "safe spots" where you can lose line-of-sight.
2.  **Sanity Socks are Key:** Always keep a supply of sanity socks. Fizzle regens sanity quickly, but in the heat of a floor five run, an instant boost can save the run.
3.  **Use the "Skinny" Advantage:** Use your small hitbox to weave through tight spaces that larger characters might struggle with. This is particularly useful when avoiding the "invisible walls" that sometimes plague specific map generations.
4.  **Team Support:** If you see a teammate being chased, use your aggro ability to pull the threat away, then lead the infected to a location far from the active machines.
