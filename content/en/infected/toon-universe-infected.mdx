<Section icon={Swords} title="Agent Skills and Abilities">

To combat the **toon universe infected**, agents are equipped with passives and actives that define their role within a team. Nimbus serves as a "Stamina Tank," capable of drawing aggro and escaping through sheer endurance. His kit is specifically designed to teach new players how to manage the sanity-stamina loop without being overly punished.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Haze">
    When your stamina drops below 100 points (50%), your movement speed increases by 30%. Use this to create distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Break Line of Sight">
    Use your increased speed to round corners. This stops the Sanity meter from depleting further.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Power Nap">
    Once in a safe spot (or in an emergency), use your active skill. You will sleep standing up for 5 seconds to fully recover stamina.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset and Extract">
    With a full stamina bar, return to the machines to continue the extraction process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Solo Play Advantage">

The distinction between machine types is also critical. In Toon Universe, "Hacking" and "Extraction" are two different skills. Hacking applies to the computer terminals (circle checks), while Extraction applies to the vats with levers. Nimbus is slightly less efficient at hacking, likely due to his oversized sweater sleeves hindering his typing speed.
