## Ability Breakdown: Sonar and Echolocation

Selena’s kit is built around the concept of "vision." In a game where the infected can hide around any corner, her ability to reveal the invisible is a game-changer. Both her passive and active abilities focus on highlighting entities through walls, providing a massive tactical advantage.

Selena’s passive ability, **Echolocation**, is an automatic pulse that triggers every 25 seconds. This pulse reveals all living life forms for 5 seconds. This is critical for maintaining awareness of teammate positions and ensuring you aren't walking into a trap set by other agents or environmental hazards.

### Active: Sonar
Her active ability, **Sonar**, is the cornerstone of her utility. With a 40-second cooldown, Selena can highlight all infected, items, and objectives temporarily for 5 seconds. 

**Key uses for Sonar include:**
*   **Objective Locating:** Instantly find PCs, extraction machines, and fuses across the map.
*   **Infected Tracking:** See exactly where entities like Patcher or Discard are moving, allowing for safe rotations.
*   **Item Scouting:** Highlight medkits, bandages, and coffee to keep your team stocked.
