## New Mechanics: The Corruption System

The most significant addition to the gameplay loop is the Corruption System. As you traverse the infected zones, your character accumulates "Static," which eventually leads to a full "Infection" state if not managed. This state reduces your movement speed but can occasionally boost your damage output at the cost of health over time.

| Mechanic | Effect | Management Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Static Accumulation** | Minor UI distortion and 5% speed reduction. | Stand near **Nature Nodes** to clear. |
| **Phantasia Fever** | Increased crit chance but 10% health drain. | Use **Antivirus Stimpacks** immediately. |
| **Glitch Step** | Random short-range teleports during combat. | Avoid high-density **Infection Pools**. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep a stack of Nature Nodes in your quick-slot bar. They are the only way to clear high-level corruption without returning to the central atrium.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough: Clearing the Central Atrium

The Central Atrium serves as the hub for the Infected Phantasia storyline. Clearing it is necessary to unlock the high-level raiding zones. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth progression through the 2026 campaign:

1.  **Initialize the Atrium Sync**: Locate the three terminal nodes in the main hall. You must defend these against waves of corrupted toons.
2.  **Calibrate Nature Nodes**: Use the "Symbiosis Tool" to link the greenery in the atrium to the central server. This reduces the area's corruption level by 40%.
3.  **Defeat the Fragmented Overseer**: This boss appears once the sync reaches 100%. Focus on the glowing blue data cores on its back to deal maximum damage.
4.  **Extract the Phantasia Core**: Once the boss is down, interact with the central pillar to receive your rewards and unlock the next sector.
