## Mastering Selena's Ability Kit

To effectively combat **toons universe monsters**, Selena players must manage their cooldowns with precision. Her abilities are not just for self-defense; they are tools for team-wide coordination.

### Echolocation (Passive)
This ability automatically triggers every 25 seconds, revealing all living life forms for 5 seconds. This passive ensures that Selena is rarely surprised by a monster lurking around a corner. It is especially useful for tracking the movement of Patcher through walls.

### Active Sonar (Active)
With a 40-second cooldown, this ability is Selena’s "panic button" and strategic tool combined. It highlights all infected, items, and objectives temporarily. 
*   **Duration**: 5 Seconds
*   **Utility**: Use this when entering a new floor or when the team is split up to locate the nearest exit or computer terminal.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Sonar does not make you invisible. While you can see the **toons universe monsters**, they can still hear your movements if you are sprinting or failing skill checks.

## Objective Strategy: Hacking vs. Extracting

In the 2026 meta, efficiency is key. Selena’s role is clearly defined by her stats. She is a "Classy Lady" who belongs at a computer terminal, not pulling heavy levers on extraction machines.

### The Hacking Workflow
1.  **Scan First**: Before touching a PC, trigger Sonar to ensure no monsters are in the immediate vicinity.
2.  **Skill Checks**: Selena’s high hacking stat makes skill checks easier. Aim for the "Blue" zones on the bar to receive progress bonuses.
3.  **Monitor Progress**: Use your recon abilities to check the completion status of other machines across the map. Selena can see the progress bars of distant objectives while her scan is active.

### Survival During Extraction
If you are forced to extract, remember that Selena’s "brittle wings" make the process slow. If a monster like Discard approaches, it is often better to abandon the machine and hide, relying on your high Sanity to stay calm, rather than trying to power through the final 5%.

## Team Synergy and Solo Play

While the developer recommends playing Selena in a team, soloing is possible for high-skill players. However, the lack of an extraction specialist makes the endgame significantly harder.

*   **With a Team**: Selena acts as the "eyes." She should call out monster movements constantly, allowing her teammates to work on extraction machines without fear.
*   **Solo Play**: Focus entirely on stealth. Use the passive Echolocation to time your movements between terminals. If you are detected by **toons universe monsters**, use your Speed 3 to reach the nearest elevator immediately.
