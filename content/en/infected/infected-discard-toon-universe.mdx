<Section icon={Brain} title="Understanding the Infected & Sanity Mechanics">

The core of the **infected discard toon universe** experience lies in the interplay between stamina and sanity. Unlike similar titles where stamina is a static resource, Toon Universe ties your physical performance to your mental state. When an Infected entity chases you, your sanity meter begins to drain. As this bar empties, your ability to recover stamina is severely penalized.

| Mechanic | Effect on Gameplay | Survival Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sanity Drain** | Slows down stamina regeneration significantly | High |
| **Infected Proximity** | Triggers sanity loss and visual distortions | Medium |
| **Malachor Fog** | Obscures vision and signals Infected presence | High |
| **Stamina Cap** | Determines how long you can sprint before exhaustion | Critical |

Currently, the game features various machines that require different skills. Understanding which agent excels at which task is the first step toward a successful extraction.

<Callout type="warning" title="Sanity Warning">

<Section icon={Map} title="Extraction Strategies & Machine Types">

In the **infected discard toon universe**, completing objectives requires balancing two different skills: Hacking and Extraction. Many players confuse these, but they are governed by different stats and machine types.

| Machine Type | Governing Stat | Interaction Style |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computers** | Hacking | Circle skill checks |
| **Vats / Levers** | Extraction | Holding interaction / Pulling |
| **Elevators** | N/A | Final escape point |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Vats First">
    Vats are usually easier to clear early in the match while sanity is high. Use agents with at least 3 points in Extraction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Sanity">
    If an Infected spotted you, hide in a locker or break line of sight immediately. Do not wait for the sanity bar to turn red.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hack Computers">
    Computers require higher focus. If you are playing Nimbus, be aware that his Hacking stat is lower, meaning skill checks will be more frequent or difficult.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Extraction">
    Once all machines are powered, head to the elevator. Use any remaining stamina to sprint, as sanity no longer matters once you exit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Grouping with an agent who has high Hacking (like certain upcoming beta characters) while you play a high-stamina agent like Nimbus creates a balanced duo.
</Callout>
