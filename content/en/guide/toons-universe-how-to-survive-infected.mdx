## Core Survival Mechanics: Stamina and Sanity

In Toons Universe, survival is dictated by two interconnected bars: Stamina and Sanity. Unlike other titles where stamina might be a fixed resource, here it is dynamic. When an infected begins a chase, your Sanity meter starts to deplete. As your sanity drops, your stamina regeneration rate slows down significantly. This creates a "death spiral" where the longer you are chased, the harder it becomes to keep running.

### Understanding the Sanity Drain

The Sanity mechanic is designed to punish players who stay in the line of sight of the infected for too long. If your sanity reaches zero, you will find your character struggling to catch their breath, making you an easy target for the corrupted toons.

| Mechanic | Effect on Gameplay | Survival Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Sanity Meter** | Depletes during chases; slows stamina regen. | Break line of sight quickly to stop the drain. |
| **Stamina Bar** | Used for sprinting and dodging. | Never let it hit 0% unless you have a recovery skill. |
| **Hacking (Computers)** | Requires "Hacking" stat; involves circle checks. | Prioritize characters with 3+ Hacking for these tasks. |
| **Extraction (Vats)** | Requires "Extraction" stat; uses lever mechanics. | Use high Extraction characters to speed up the final phase. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore the Sanity meter. If your sanity is low, your stamina will not recover fast enough to escape a second encounter. Find a safe zone to let your Sanity stabilize before approaching the next objective.

## Tactical Guide: How to Survive the Infected

To survive an encounter, you must understand the two main objective types: Hacking and Extraction. In Toons Universe, these are governed by different stats. Computers require the Hacking skill and involve "circle checks," while the vats with levers require the Extraction skill.

### Step-by-Step Escape Plan

1.  **Identify the Threat:** Listen for audio cues. Each infected toon has unique sounds and voice lines.
2.  **Manage the Sanity Drain:** As soon as a chase begins, look for corners, doors, or obstacles. Breaking the line of sight is the only way to stop your Sanity from plummeting.
3.  **Use the Environment:** Use the layout of the map to your advantage. If you are playing as Nimbus, wait for your stamina to drop below 50% to trigger the **Haze** speed boost before making a sharp turn into a hiding spot.
4.  **The Power Nap Reset:** If you are cornered or your Sanity is so low that your stamina won't refill, find a brief window of safety (like a closet or a far corner) and trigger **Power Nap**. Those 5 seconds of vulnerability are worth the 100% stamina reset.

> **💡 Tip:** When working in a team, have the player with the highest Hacking stat handle the computers while the high-stamina characters (like Nimbus) act as decoys to draw the infected away.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the difference between Hacking and Extraction in Toons Universe?**

A: Hacking is a stat used specifically for computer terminals that involve circle-timing mini-games. Extraction is a stat used for the vats and levers found throughout the levels. Characters usually excel in one or the other, so check your stats before approaching an objective.

**Q: How do I stop my Sanity from dropping during a chase?**

A: The only way to stop Sanity loss is to break the line of sight with the infected. Once the infected can no longer see you, the drain stops, though your stamina regeneration will remain slow until your Sanity naturally recovers in a safe area.

**Q: Is Nimbus the best character for beginners to survive the infected?**

A: Yes, Nimbus is highly recommended for beginners. His high stamina pool and the ability to fully restore it with **Power Nap** make him very forgiving. Additionally, his **Haze** passive provides a speed boost when you are most vulnerable, helping you learn **toons universe how to survive infected** without being punished too harshly for mistakes.

**Q: Can the infected enter hiding spots like lockers or closets?**

A: While most basic infected cannot enter hiding spots, some advanced variants in the 2026 updates have the ability to "sniff out" players who stay hidden for too long. Always use hiding spots as a temporary measure to reset your Sanity, rather than a permanent solution.
