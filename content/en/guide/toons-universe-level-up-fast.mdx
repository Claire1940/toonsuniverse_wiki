### The Chase Mechanic Meta
The most lucrative way to earn XP is through the chase mechanic. Unlike other tasks that give a flat reward, chases provide incremental XP based on how long you can maintain the threat's attention. This rewards high-skill movement and map knowledge.

The increments for chase XP are as follows:
1. **First 15 seconds:** 15 XP
2. **Next 15 seconds:** 20 XP
3. **Following 30 seconds:** 25 XP
4. **Final 30 seconds:** 30 XP

For a full 90-second chase, you earn a staggering **90 XP**. This is significantly more than completing several machines combined. If you are playing a fast character like **Fantasia** or **Fizzle**, your main objective to **toons universe level up fast** should be to engage in a chase for at least 90 seconds, then safely disengage to help with the main machine.

## Solo Grinding vs. Team Play

While Toons Universe is designed as a multiplayer experience, the most efficient way to grind levels is actually through solo play. When playing in a full lobby, you are competing with teammates for machines, fuses, and chase time. In a solo environment, every single point of XP available on the floor is yours for the taking.

**Why Solo Play is Superior for Leveling:**
*   **Total Machine Control:** You can complete every machine yourself, ensuring you get the 10 XP per machine and the 20 XP for the main machine.
*   **Event Monopoly:** During elevator breakdowns or blackouts, you are the only one available to fix the issues, guaranteeing the 15 XP rewards.
*   **Guaranteed Chases:** The threat has no one else to target, meaning you can easily manipulate the AI to maintain a 90-second chase.
*   **Ability Farming:** You have the time and safety to ensure you use your abilities the required three times per floor without pressure.
