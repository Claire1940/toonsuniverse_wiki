<Section icon={Swords} title="S-Tier & A-Tier Emblem Deep Dive">

The top-tier emblems are those that offer benefits regardless of the floor layout or team composition. The **Tadpole Emblem** (Tibo) is arguably the strongest item in the game. Because its 15% stamina boost stacks, a full team of 10 players equipping it would grant everyone a 150% stamina increase. This transforms even the lowest stamina characters into marathon runners. 

Similarly, the **Bat Emblem** (Selina) provides a 15% speed boost to all objectives. Unlike other emblems that only target "extractors" or "computers," the Bat Emblem covers both. This makes it a universal pick for any agent tasked with clearing machines. For distractors, the **Sheep Emblem** (Nimbus) is the gold standard, reducing stamina consumption by 30%, which effectively increases your chase time by nearly a third.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stacking Limits">
While most team buffs stack, always coordinate with your party. Having too many utility emblems (like Snake) might be redundant if the team already knows the machine spawns.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock and Level Up Agents">

Unlocking the best items on the toons universe tier list requires a dedicated grind to Level 100. The process is straightforward but requires consistent play. Follow these steps to maximize your XP gain per session.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Target Agent">
    Select the agent whose emblem you want first. We recommend starting with Tibo (Tadpole) or Patcher (Bear) for early survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Focus on Objectives">
    Completing machines (Computers and Extractors) provides the most consistent XP. If you are playing a character with low extraction stats, stay near teammates to assist.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Engage in Chases">
    Distracting the infected grants XP based on the duration of the chase. Use agents like Fantasia or Fizzle to lead enemies away from machines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Your Progress">
    Regularly check the "Agent Info" tab. Click the flag icon and scroll to the bottom to see your current level and how close you are to the Level 100 milestone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="XP Scaling">
XP gain does not slow down significantly at higher levels. Most players report gaining roughly one level per floor if they are actively participating in objectives.
