## What is the Toons Universe Discard Emblem?

The **toons universe discard emblem** is the signature reward for reaching the level cap with the agent Discard. Formally known as the **Dolphin Emblem**, its primary function is to provide a buff to the entire team regarding item efficiency. Specifically, it increases the effectiveness of items that boost hacking and extracting by **20%**.

Unlike passive emblems that provide a flat speed increase to machines, this emblem is contingent on the players actually possessing and using specific items found throughout the floors. This makes it a "multiplier" style of equipment. If your team is diligent about looting and utilizing hacking/extracting boosters, the Dolphin Emblem can theoretically shorten floor times significantly. However, in the 2026 meta, most players tend to prioritize healing items in their limited inventory slots, which often puts this emblem at a disadvantage.

## How to Unlock Emblems in Toons Universe

Unlocking the **toons universe discard emblem** is not an overnight task. In the current 2026 version of the game, there is only one path to obtaining these powerful items: leveling your agents. To earn the Dolphin Emblem, you must grind Discard all the way to **Level 100**.

While Level 100 sounds daunting, players earn experience points (XP) through almost every action in the game. You gain XP by:
*   Completing objectives (Computers and Extractors)
*   Finishing floors successfully
*   Engaging in and escaping from chases
*   Using character-specific abilities

Experienced players can often gain one level per floor if they are efficient with machines. If you find yourself struggling to reach machines because of high-threat infected, consider playing a distractor role to keep your XP gains consistent. Once you hit the level cap, you can equip the emblem by selecting the agent, clicking "Info," and navigating to the flag icon at the bottom of the menu.

In the broader context of Toons Universe equipment, the **toons universe discard emblem** is generally ranked in the **C Tier**. While it isn't "dumpster tier," it faces stiff competition from emblems that provide more reliable, non-situational benefits.

The main issue with the Dolphin Emblem is its reliance on items. In a standard run, players often face a "medkit economy" problem where they must choose between a speed boost or a life-saving heal. Most players choose the heal. Furthermore, the 20% bonus is considered relatively low unless it is combined with other speed-boosting emblems like Selina's Bat Emblem.

## Comparing Discard to Other Objective Emblems

When deciding whether to run the **toons universe discard emblem**, you must compare it to the "Big Two" of objective speed: Squiddle’s Squid Emblem and Selina’s Bat Emblem. 

Squiddle's emblem gives a 15% head start specifically on extraction (lever) machines. Selina's Bat Emblem, which is widely considered the gold standard, gives a flat 15% speed increase to *all* objectives (computers and extractors). Because Selina's boost is always active and doesn't require an item slot, it is almost always preferred over Discard's Dolphin Emblem.

However, if a team is dedicated to a "Speedrun" build, they might stack the following:
1.  **Selina's Bat Emblem** (Passive speed)
2.  **Discard's Dolphin Emblem** (Boosted item speed)
3.  **Hacking/Extracting Items** (The base boost)

In this specific scenario, the interaction between the items and the **toons universe discard emblem** can result in machines being completed in record time, which is vital for high-difficulty Malicor farming.

## Strategic Synergy and Team Compositions

To make the most of the **toons universe discard emblem**, your team composition needs to be intentional. This is not an emblem for solo players or "pick-up groups" where communication is low. It shines in environments where one player acts as the designated "Logistician."

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not equip the Dolphin Emblem if you do not plan on looting chests. Its value drops to zero if your team finishes a floor without using a single objective-boosting item.

### Recommended Team Roles
*   **The Distractor:** Usually a Nimbus or Fizzle. They don't need the Discard boost as they are rarely on machines.
*   **The Objective Specialist:** A Rossum or Guido. These agents should be the ones receiving the boosted items.
*   **The Support:** An agent equipped with the **toons universe discard emblem** to ensure the Specialist's items are 20% more effective.

The stacking nature of emblems is still a point of discussion among the community. While the developers have confirmed that the Tadpole emblem stacks, it is currently believed that the Dolphin Emblem's 20% bonus applies to the *base value* of the item used. If multiple players equip the Discard emblem, the math suggests a significant diminishing return unless the developers adjust the stacking mechanics in a future 2026 patch.

## Is the Discard Emblem Worth the Grind?

For the average player, the **toons universe discard emblem** should be a secondary priority. Your first goals should be unlocking the Tadpole (Tibo) for stamina or the Bat (Selina) for universal speed. Discard is a fun agent to play, but his emblem requires a very specific playstyle to be viable.

If you are a completionist or a member of a competitive speedrunning clan, then reaching Level 100 with Discard is mandatory. The 20% item boost is the only way to reach the absolute maximum theoretical "Objective Speed" in the game. For everyone else, it remains a situational C-tier tool that is often overshadowed by more consistent passive buffs.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Toons Universe Discard Emblem work on both computers and extractors?**

A: Yes. As long as the item you are using is designed to boost either hacking (computers) or extracting (levers), the 20% bonus from the Dolphin Emblem will apply.

**Q: Can I equip the Discard emblem on other agents once I unlock it?**

A: Absolutely. Once you reach Level 100 with Discard and unlock the emblem, you can equip it on any agent in your roster. You are limited to two emblem slots per agent.

**Q: Why is the Toons Universe Discard Emblem ranked lower than Selina's Bat Emblem?**

A: The **toons universe discard emblem** requires you to find and use items to see any benefit. Selina's Bat Emblem provides a 15% speed boost at all times, regardless of your inventory, making it much more reliable for general play.

**Q: Does the 20% boost apply to the whole team or just the wearer?**

A: The Dolphin Emblem provides a team-wide buff. If you have it equipped, any teammate who uses a hacking or extracting booster will benefit from the 20% increased effectiveness.
