## Toons Universe Does Discard Emblem Stack?

The short answer is: yes, but with specific caveats regarding the "Source Type." In Toons Universe, the game engine distinguishes between active ability triggers and passive equipment triggers. If you are wondering if the **toons universe does discard emblem stack** when using two identical items, the answer is usually no. However, if you are stacking a Discard Emblem from your talent tree with a Discard Emblem from your gear, the effects are additive.

For example, if your Talent Tree provides a 5% damage boost per discarded card and your equipped Emblem provides a flat +10 damage, the game calculates the flat bonus first, followed by the percentage increase. This multi-layered approach is what allows "Discard Burn" decks to remain viable in the 2026 competitive ladder.

### Additive vs. Multiplicative Stacking

Understanding the difference between additive and multiplicative stacking is the hallmark of a pro player. Toons Universe primarily uses additive stacking for flat stats and multiplicative stacking for duration-based buffs.

1.  **Flat Stat Stacking (Additive):** If you have two sources of +5 Attack, you get +10.
2.  **Percentage Stacking (Multiplicative):** If you have two sources of 10% damage increase, the formula is `Base * 1.10 * 1.10`, resulting in a 21% increase rather than 20%.
