## How to Unlock Emblems in Toons Universe

Unlike other games in the genre that may rely on random drops or shop purchases, Toons Universe utilizes a mastery-based system. Every agent in the game has a unique emblem tied specifically to their progression. 

To obtain an emblem, you must reach **Level 100** with that specific agent. While a hundred levels may sound daunting to a new player, the experience gain in Toons Universe is relatively generous compared to similar titles. Players earn Experience Points (XP) by performing various in-game actions that contribute to the team's success.

### Efficient XP Farming Methods

To reach Level 100 quickly, focus on the following activities during your runs:

*   **Objective Completion:** Finishing machines (computers and extractors) provides the most consistent XP.
*   **Floor Completion:** Simply surviving and moving to the next floor grants a flat XP bonus.
*   **Chase Interaction:** Agents like Fantasia or Fizzle can gain significant XP by distracting the infected. Leading the infected on long chases is a high-risk, high-reward method for leveling.
*   **Action Variety:** Using medkits, finding items, and interacting with environmental hazards all contribute small amounts to your total progression.

## Detailed Emblem Breakdown

### The Power of Stacking: Tibo’s Tadpole Emblem
The Tadpole Emblem is arguably the strongest item in the game due to its stacking mechanic. In a standard 10-player lobby, if every player equips the Tadpole Emblem, the entire team receives a massive 150% boost to their maximum stamina. This allows even the slowest characters to outrun threats for extended periods.

### Objective Efficiency: Selina’s Bat Emblem
Speed is the most important factor for earning [Malicor](https://www.roblox.com/games/17255265103/Toons-Universe) and progressing through the game. The Bat Emblem increases speed on all objectives by 15%. Because it applies to both computer hacking and lever extraction, it is more versatile than Squiddle’s Squid Emblem, which only targets extraction speed.

### Survival and Healing: Patcher vs. Fantasia
Choosing between the Bear and Unicorn emblems depends on your team dynamic:
1.  **Bear Emblem (Patcher):** Better for team play because the items generated can be dropped and shared with others.
2.  **Unicorn Emblem (Fantasia):** Better for solo play as it provides a direct health restoration to the user without occupying an inventory slot.

## Recommended Unlock Order

When planning your journey in **toons universe how to get emblems**, the order in which you level your agents can significantly impact your efficiency. We recommend the following progression path:

1.  **Tibo (Tadpole):** Start here. The stamina boost is the most noticeable upgrade for a new player.
2.  **Guido & Rossom (Snake):** Unlock this next to help you learn the machine spawn locations.
3.  **Patcher (Bear):** Provides a safety net of healing items for longer runs.
4.  **Selina (Bat):** Once you have unlocked Selina, focus entirely on her emblem to maximize your objective speed for the endgame.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be aware that some emblems, like the Lizard Emblem (Fizzle), only provide benefits for the first two minutes of a floor. If you are not a dedicated distractor, these slots are better used for permanent stat boosts.

## Synergy and Equipment Slots
Every agent can equip exactly two emblems. This allows for powerful combinations. For example, combining the **Sheep Emblem** with the **Tadpole Emblem** creates a "Stamina God" build, where you have a massive pool of energy and consume it 30% slower. 

Alternatively, an "Objective Specialist" might combine the **Bat Emblem** with the **Fish Emblem**, allowing them to finish machines quickly and have their abilities ready for the next task almost immediately.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Toons Universe how to get emblems process require spending Malicor?**

A: No, emblems are earned strictly through gameplay by reaching Level 100 with an agent. However, you will need Malicor to purchase the agents themselves before you can begin leveling them.

**Q: Can I use one agent's emblem on a different agent?**

A: Yes. Once you unlock an emblem by reaching Level 100 with an agent, that emblem becomes available for any other agent in your roster to equip.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up an agent to 100?**

A: The fastest method is a combination of completing objectives and surviving long chases. Playing with a coordinated team that allows you to finish multiple machines per floor will significantly speed up the process.

**Q: Do emblem effects stack if multiple people wear them?**

A: Some do and some don't. The Tadpole Emblem is confirmed to stack team-wide. Others, like the Bat Emblem, generally provide an individual boost unless the description specifically mentions a team-wide effect.
