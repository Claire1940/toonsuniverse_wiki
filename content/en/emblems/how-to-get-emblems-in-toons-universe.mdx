### Detailed Analysis of Top Emblems

The **Tadpole Emblem** is widely considered the best in the game because its effect is multiplicative and team-wide. If all ten players in a lobby equip the Tadpole, the entire team receives a staggering 150% increase to their maximum stamina. This allows even low-stamina characters to outrun the most aggressive infected for extended periods.

The **Bat Emblem** is the ultimate efficiency tool. Unlike the Squid emblem, which only works on lever-based extractors, the Bat emblem increases speed on *all* objectives. This reduces the time spent vulnerable at a machine and speeds up the overall floor progression, leading to faster Malicor farming.
